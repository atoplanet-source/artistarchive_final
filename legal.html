Legal Information

Last updated: February 2025

Company Information

Artist Archives is a visual data licensing company specializing in ethically sourced, rights-cleared photography and video for AI training, machine learning, and creative applications.

Copyright & Content Ownership

All content within the Artist Archives dataset is sourced through direct agreements with professional photographers and rights holders. Each asset is:

  • Contractually licensed from the original creator or rights holder
  • Cleared for AI training and machine learning applications
  • Subject to specific usage terms defined in licensing agreements
  • Protected by copyright and intellectual property laws

Rights Clearance

Artist Archives maintains comprehensive documentation for all content, including:

  • Proof of ownership or licensing rights from original creators
  • Model releases where applicable
  • Property releases where applicable
  • Chain of title documentation

Permitted Uses

Licensed content from Artist Archives may be used for:

  • Training machine learning and AI models
  • Fine-tuning foundation models
  • Safety testing and benchmarking
  • Research and development
  • Other uses as specified in individual licensing agreements

Prohibited Uses

Without explicit written permission, licensed content may not be:

  • Redistributed or resold to third parties
  • Used to create competing datasets
  • Used in ways that violate applicable laws
  • Used beyond the scope of the licensing agreement

DMCA & Takedown Requests

If you believe any content in our archive infringes on your copyright, please contact us with detailed information about the alleged infringement. We take all claims seriously and will respond promptly to valid requests.

Creator Partnerships

Artist Archives works directly with photographers and creators, ensuring fair compensation and transparent terms. If you are a professional photographer interested in contributing to our archive, please contact us through our website.

Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from the use of Artist Archives content or services shall be resolved through binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association, unless otherwise specified in individual licensing agreements.

Contact

For legal inquiries, licensing questions, or to report potential issues, please contact us through our website contact form.
